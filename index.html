User Manual (.pdf)

Super Duck Invaders is a fast-paced action game where The University of York has been invaded by ducks from outta space!!! It's upto to you , the only earthling-duck left standing, to take back your home. Who will be the last duck standing?

Fly, Swim, Waddle-Waddle

your way through 8 levels filled with randomly generated enemies. Complete three different objectives: defeat the enemies, capture the flag or survive the onslaught to unlock levels.

Shoot 'N' Slash

your way to victory with you trusty gun and saber. Mastering these weapons means you can deflect enemy bullets and even shoot them down in mid-air!

Power Up

with various items gained from defeating enemies. Items give you abilities to achieve a higher score!

System Requirements (Minimum)

  • OS: Windows 7 onwards/Linux
  • Disk space: 12.5 MB
